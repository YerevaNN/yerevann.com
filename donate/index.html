Reasearch Support Programs

Supporting Armenia's ML research ecosystem through competitive fellowships and scholarship opportunities - from early-career internships to established researcher positions.

Named Annual Fellowship for Senior Researchers


1 year program: $20,000 (on top of government funding)

  1. For established ML researchers
  2. Enables full-time research focus
  3. Covers research salary, conference participation (at least one/ year) and health insurance


Named Annual Scholarship for Junior Researchers


1 year program: $10,000 (on top of government funding)

  1. For promising CS/ML graduates
  2. Enables full-time research focus
  3. Covers research salary, conference participation (at least one/ year) and health insurance

Multiply Your Impact Through Corporate Gift Matching


Many employers in the US match your charitable contributions, doubling or tripling the impact of your gift.


If you work for Adobe, Google, Netflix Microsoft, or any company associated with the Benevity platform, your contribution could qualify.


Here's How It Works:


  1. Make your donation to YerevaNN by searching “YerevaNN: Support Non-profit ML Research” on Benevity platform. The charity campaign for YerevaNN is managed by the reArmenia (a US-registered 501(c)3 nonprofit).
  2. Fill out your company's matching gift form and submit it as per their instructions.
