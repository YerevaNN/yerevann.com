YerevaNN

YerevaNN /jɛɾɛvɑnˈɛn/ is a machine learning research center based in Yerevan, Armenia. Listen to the EVN Report’s podcast to learn about our lab’s recent research directions and their strategic importance for Armenia.

Latest Research

Small Molecule Optimization with Large Language Models. Up to 2B parameter language models (Chemlactica and Chemma) combined with a genetic algorithm produces state-of-the-art results on most molecular optimization benchmarks.

PreprintModelsCode

Research Directions

We focus on advancing large language models (LLMs) in biotech and robotics—two fields with transformative potential. These areas, recognized by Y Combinator as the next frontiers, offer us an exciting opportunity to make an impact in selected fields.

AI for Molecule Generation

We are developing an advanced AI-driven molecular “programming language” to revolutionize drug discovery by enabling precise, programmatic design of pharmaceutical compounds.

Read more
AI for Robotics

We're pioneering advancements in artificial intelligence to enhance robotic capabilities. This direction focuses on predicting the environment's 3D model based on high-frequency signals.
